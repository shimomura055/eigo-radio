単語コーナー(vocab_intro)

Before we start, here are five useful words for today.

Radar: equipment that finds objects from far away. Coastal radar can see ships near the shore.

Blockade: action that stops ships or goods moving. The blockade could stop ships from entering the area.

Facility: a place built for a special job. The strikes hit a military facility near the coast.

Reroute: to send something by a different way. Some ships may reroute to avoid danger.

Cargo: goods carried by a ship or plane. The ship was carrying cargo through the region.

MAYA: The U.S. is bombing Iran again? Now I see robot boats everywhere in the headlines. I thought there was a ceasefire, but it seems to be breaking apart.
LEO: Yeah. The key is not only that fighting returned. Look at what the latest strikes were aimed at.
MAYA: So what did the U.S. actually hit this time?
LEO: On July 13, U.S. forces hit Iranian air defenses, coastal radar, missile and drone sites, and small boats. That is a big list. They were not just hitting one building after making an announcement. They hit the things Iran uses near the coast to see trouble, protect itself, and send weapons out. So this is a turning point. It is not background noise around the blockade news. The strikes show that the U.S. is going after Iran's ability to work at sea, right now. That is why this latest wave matters more than a simple warning.
MAYA: But maybe this blockade is still mostly a political message, you know? Maybe it is more talk than action.
LEO: No, not really. The blockade announcement is not the whole story. The U.S. is also striking the tools Iran uses to watch the coast, defend it, and threaten ships there. That is more than talk. The message is moving from words toward control of the water. I mean, they are not only saying, "Stay back." They are trying to decide who can move safely there now.
MAYA: So where do the robot boats fit into all this?
LEO: They fit into it as a way to hit Iran's naval support from the water. Reports say this was the first U.S. use of one-way attack sea drones in this conflict. The drones went after an Iranian ship-maintenance facility and a submarine. That is the sharp new part. The target was not only a weapon sitting outside somewhere. It was part of the place that keeps ships working. It helps fix them, ready them, and send them back out. That matters because ships and submarines need people, parts, and safe places to return after a fight.

LEO: Exactly. That is the unsettling part. A small sea drone can make the first trip without putting a U.S. crew in that boat. But it can still reach the machines that keep ships and submarines moving. So now we are asking who can control the water, not only who can fire from farther away.
MAYA: Can Washington still call this pressure if it says it will keep the strait open? I vaguely remember an older attack on a ship, and things got worse.

Before this conflict, roughly one-fifth of the world's oil and gas went through it. So pressure there tests who can shape that route. America wants to help set the rules. Iran says Washington cannot set those rules. And everybody using the water has to watch what happens next.

5. 発生した問題と対処

① 文字コードエラー(即解決)

解決済み — gather_topic.py実行時、Windows既定のcp932コンソールエンコーディングでUnicode文字(en dash等)が扱えずクラッシュ。PYTHONIOENCODING=utf-8 / PYTHONUTF8=1を環境変数として指定して再実行し、解決。コード自体は変更していない。

② dotenvモジュール未検出(即解決)

解決済み — システムPythonではなく仮想環境(.venv)のPythonを使う必要があった。.venv/Scripts/python.exeを明示的に使用することで解決。コード変更なし。

③ tts_test.pyが約38分間ハング(重大)

原因調査・改善対応済み

  • 症状: 初回のTTS実行で、開始から38分経過してもプロセスが完了せず、CPU使用量・標準出力に一切変化がなかった
  • 調査結果: Google宛のTCP接続(172.217.117.4:443)がEstablishedのまま変化せず継続。google-genai SDK(v2.11.0)のソースを確認したところ、tts_test.pyはAPI呼び出しに一切タイムアウトを指定しておらず、SDKのデフォルト挙動によりtimeout=Noneがhttpxに渡され、無期限待ちになっていたことが判明
  • 対処: ユーザー承認のもとプロセスを強制終了 → 途中生成ファイルなしを確認 → 恒久対策として以下2点を実装
    • 各TTS API呼び出し(vocab_intro / 対話チャンク10回)にチャンク単位の所要時間計測ログを追加(flush=True付きで、ハング時にもログが即座に書き出されるように)
    • generate_content呼び出しに120秒のタイムアウト(http_options=types.HttpOptions(timeout=120_000))を追加。タイムアウト例外は既存のexcept Exceptionで捕捉され、既存のMAX_RETRY=2によるリトライ対象に自動的に含まれることをSDK内部実装から確認済み
  • 再実行結果: ログ追加後に再実行したところ、全11回のAPI呼び出しは10.4〜42.4秒(平均約27秒)で正常完了し、合計約5.9分でWAVファイルが生成された。今回のハングは異常な単発事象だったことが定量的に裏付けられた
